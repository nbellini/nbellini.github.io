ABOUT THE COMPANY

Original Compositions for Any Project

The Crew Collective was founded in 2021 around the idea that all media needs a sound. Formed by 5 Berklee College of Music graduates, The Crew Collective focuses on in-house writing, arranging, and production to create original works of music for projects ranging from TikToks and jingles to commercials and TV, and everything else in between. Currently based in Nashville, TN, The Crew Songwriting Collective is writing like never before thanks to Music City, and are excited to create and compose works like never before.

WRITERS

The ones behind the music

Nikki Bellini

Founder & Writer

The 'business-head' of The Crew, Nikki dual majored in music business and songwriting at Berklee; once the idea of a songwriting collective was formed, Nikki saw it as an opportunity to pursue both of her passions. Bringing both her business knowledge and songwriting abilities in toplining and lyric writing, Nikki is making her dreams a reality with The Crew Collective.

Francis McCarthy

Writer

The 'friendly-giant' of The Crew, Francis is a true creator. Following his passions for both writing and photography, Francis looks to create in all aspects of his life. With The Crew Collective, Francis is bringing his unique sense of lyricism, poetry, and production to each project, and will likely sneak some beatboxing into his tracks.

Shannon Sperl

Writer & Artist

Shannon, otherwise known as the 'cool-mom' of the group, is the passionate and caring firecracker of The Crew, and wants to pursue her dreams of performing, writing, and recording. Bringing her own pop and country influences, Shannon offers the pop edge of the group, and the spirit to get things done.

PRODUCERS

The ones behind the audio

Thomas Murphy

Producer & Editor

Thomas, the 'class-clown' of The Crew, is the Beatles-loving, 70's child we all know and love. With expertise in audio recording, editing, and instrumentation, Thomas brings The Crew Collective to new recording heights, and always brings new and unique ideas to the process.

Sterling Bauer

Producer & Mixer

The 'free-spirit' of The Crew, Sterling is interested in everything from gear and production to writing and performance. Having produced, mixed, and mastered on many projects in recent years, Sterling is excited to bring his smooth sound, technique, and style to The Crew Collective.

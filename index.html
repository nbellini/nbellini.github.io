ABOUT THE COMPANY

Original Compositions for Any Project

The Crew Collective was founded in 2021 around the idea that all media needs a sound. Formed by 5 Berklee College of Music graduates, The Crew Collective focuses on in-house writing, arranging, and production to create original works of music for projects ranging from TikTok to jingles to commercials and everything in between. Currently based in Nashville, TN, The Crew is expanding their Songwriting Collective like never before in Music City, and continues to create every day.
